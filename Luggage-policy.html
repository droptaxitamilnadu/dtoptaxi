Luggage Policy

At Drop Taxi Tamilnadu, we aim to provide comfortable and safe travel experiences across our Drop Taxi, One-way Taxi, and Tour Package services. To ensure convenience for all passengers, we have set the following luggage policy based on vehicle type:

1. Sedan (e.g., Etios, Dzire, Amaze)

  • Maximum of 2 medium-sized suitcases (20–25 kg each) + 1 small cabin bag is permitted.
  • Luggage must fit inside the car boot only.
  • Carrying luggage inside the passenger cabin or on the roof carrier will attract an additional charge of ₹300.
  • Oversized luggage (e.g., large boxes, furniture, appliances) is not allowed in Sedans.

2. SUV (e.g., Innova, Ertiga, Xylo, Crysta)

  • Maximum of 4 medium-sized suitcases (20–25 kg each) + 2 small cabin bags is permitted.
  • Luggage should be arranged in the boot space.
  • If luggage is placed inside the passenger cabin or on the roof carrier, an additional charge of ₹300 will apply.
  • SUVs are more suitable for family trips and tour packages with extra luggage needs.

3. General Guidelines

  • Customers must ensure luggage is packed securely. Drop Taxi Tamilnadu is not responsible for damage to fragile or improperly packed items.
  • Hazardous, flammable, or illegal items are strictly prohibited.
  • If you have extra luggage beyond the prescribed limits, please book an SUV for a safe and comfortable journey.
  • The driver has the right to refuse unsafe or excess luggage that may affect passenger safety or vehicle performance.

4. Travel Tips

  • Always inform Drop Taxi Tamilnadu of your luggage requirements at the time of booking to assign the most suitable vehicle.
  • For long trips or tour packages, we recommend booking an SUV if you are carrying more than 2 large bags.
